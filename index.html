Who is Josh Hamman?

A creative mind driven by challenge and impact.

My name is Josh Hamman and Im a Design Strategist. Equiped with a Bachlors of Science in Graphic Design and a fiery passion for learning; Ive found myself constantly consuming information and generating art from those learning expieriences. Because of this I've found myself in possession of a very wide body of surface knowledge that is just always growing and in tandem with the values of really listening, watching, and understanding; allows me to bring a very deep toolbox to the table when combating different design challenges & problems.
