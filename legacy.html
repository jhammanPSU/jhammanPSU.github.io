Magazine Type Design // The Legacy of Invention

Charles and Ray Eames are two very well known figure heads in the world of design.

With talents ranging from paintings to photographs, interior and exterior design, the Eames tried and excelled in it all. One of the great designs they came up with was the Eames lounge chair. While being elegant, modern, and classic, all at the same time, it’s no wonder why these loungers have made a notorious name for themselves as being the pinnacle of comfort and design.

Lounging About, is a magazine that is intended to highlight and showcase such chairs. Touching briefly on the history of it’s creators, the magazine mainly presents the benefits of such a chair in ones home or office. As an easily accessible upgrade to ones quality of life.

The Eames lounge chair is available in practically any color or fabric allowing it to fit perfectly within any and all interior environments. Read about why you should buy an Eames lounge chair and make strides for a relaxing future today.
