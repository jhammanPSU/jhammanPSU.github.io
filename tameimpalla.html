Vynil Album Art

Tame Impala, a band originally from Australia, is famed by millions as one of the best psychedelic rock bands currently playing.

Part of what gives them their unique ability to capture the attention of so many, is their ability to change the simplest beats in a way that is not only unexpected but slightly out of place while maintaining its original roots to familiar notes. The re-design of their “Inner Speaker” album pays tribute to this key characteristics of the band.

A simple, calm photograph sets the familiar mood of peaceful music while one big change to the front of the cover allows leverage to their ability to make unexpected changes that still fit in.

The upside down portion of the picture embodies that change by being unexpectedly different from the rest of the photo while still seamlessly fitting the part that it is normal and a part of the rest of the photo.
